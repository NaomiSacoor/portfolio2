VINYL DESIGN

SUN ELECTRIC - KITCHEN

For a school project, I was assigned to design the vinyl for a record of choice. I designed a cover, sleeve, and labels for the techno record Kitchen by Sun Electric, signed with R&S Records. I created patterns to capture the rythmic metallic sounds in the beat.

VINYL DESIGN

OBLIQUE STRATEGIES - SONGS  OF BRITISH BIRDS - COBBLESTONESTREET

For a school project, I was assigned to design the vinyl for an imaginary record of choice. I designed a cover, sleeve, and labels for the record Cobblestone Street by Mike Tramp signed with Cleopatra Records. I created a font to capture the rythmic, repeatedly sounds of driving over cobblestones. The two other albums 'Oblique Strategies' and 'Songs Of British Birds' are named after strategies for creative people as a source of ispiration.

DINGBAT DESIGN

NEWSPAPER DONALD TRUMP

For a school project, I was assigned to design a dingbat font to use in a newspaper about Donald Trump. I was inspired by the amateuristic font the womens march protesters used and loved how they got so creative with them. The newspaper itself will be up soon.
